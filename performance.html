What serious traders should verify

Before deploying any automated trading robot on a live account, rigorous due diligence is essential. A profitable backtest or a few winning weeks on a demo account are not enough to confirm the reliability of an algorithmic strategy. The nature of gold (XAUUSD) trading — with its rapid price swings, sensitivity to macroeconomic news, and variable spread conditions — demands that every trader validate the system under realistic market scenarios.

A responsible approach to automated trading 24/5 includes verifying how the EA handles drawdown, lot sizing relative to account equity, and execution quality during high-impact news events. The checklist below outlines the verification steps that separate disciplined traders from those who take unnecessary risks.

  • Use demo testing first
  • Match lot sizing to account balance
  • Understand grid exposure
  • Review spread and broker conditions
  • Monitor performance during volatile gold sessions
